About Shamrock Mechanical

Practical mechanical work for commercial and industrial clients.

Shamrock Mechanical is based in Granton, Ontario and works with commercial and industrial clients on HVAC, refrigeration, ductwork, gas fitting, retrofits, service, and design support.

Who we are

Shamrock Mechanical focuses on mechanical systems that need more than a generic service call. The work often involves site conditions, equipment coordination, timing, access, and practical problem solving.

Our work includes

  • Commercial and industrial HVAC
  • Tendered projects and design-build work
  • Retrofit HVAC units and systems
  • Refrigeration, VRF systems, and heat pumps
  • Ductwork, custom fabrication, and installation
  • Gas fitting, piping, venting, and breeching
  • Duct design and building system consulting
